Chaos Theory

Part of Spring 2025 Semester. Registrations open in January 2025.

Chaos is not randomness. It is a break in the link between cause and effect. When effect cannot be tidily traced back to a singular cause, predicting the future becomes challenging. Assuming rational economic agents driving human nature, and abstracting from unpredictable and irrational behaviour has left gaping areas for research.

The Science of Creativity

Part of Spring 2025 Semester. Registrations open in January 2025.

This course provides a comprehensive overview of past and current research on creative thinking. The sessions will cover theories of creativity and the behavioural and neural basis of creativity. Finally, each session will provide empirically grounded suggestions on how to stimulate your own creative thinking.
