Participate

There are 5 ways to participate at Shoshin College.

1. Spaces and Venues

Help us find venues for our courses. Each course has different space and equipment requirements. We are on the look out for spaces that enable seminars, workshops, presentations usually with capacity of up to 10-15 people.

Finding free or low-cost spaces in London is hard so this is one of the most important things one can contribute to.

2. Design

Do you like to design? We're always on the lookout for creative ways to reshape our website, our brand identity, our marketing material.

Here's a fun challenge one could start with: design a poster for one of the courses being offered.

3. Marketing

There are a lot of people who would be interested in Shoshin College if they had heard of it. Help us spread the word! We're running online ads, distributing posters and flyers around London, and talking to new and old friends who would be into this.

4. Teach a Course
